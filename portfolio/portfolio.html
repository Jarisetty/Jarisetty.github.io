Academic Writing

As an English major, I have produced countless analytical papers over my academic career. I've refined my data collection and resource evaluation skills through research projects that required sorting through numerous sources in a limited window of time. I've become adroit at the kind of critical thinking required by complex texts and can expertly employ that thinking in a variety of ways. This index contains those works which I consider emblematic of my skills.

Creative Writing

While many people may disdain creative writing as trivial, the art in fact demands a great deal of skill and intelligence to perform. Quality prose and poetry requires flexible thinking, ingenuity, and attention to detail, among other necessities in any craft such as knowledge of its history and techniques. The pieces included in this collected display my proficiency in all those requirements.
