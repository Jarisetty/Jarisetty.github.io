Prison Policy Initiative Research Project (PDF)

To demonstrate the research I did on the Prison Policy Initiative, I assembled this presentation and short overview of the organization. This research was completed for the Integrative Experience course Going to Jail. I focused on analyzing the goals of the organization and the methods used to acheive them, as well as evaluating those goals and methods. I was previously familiar with the PPI as part of my Criminolgy class, so this project built off that previous familiarity. I summarized the group's goals and looked through their website to establish basis for the methods they used, and then assessed them on validity and effectiveness.
