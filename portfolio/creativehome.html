I experimented with dynamic form for these pieces, as well as with obscure diction. Style Imitation Letter (PDF)

For the class Victorian Monstrosity, I wrote a creative work in the style of Sheridan Le Fanu's "Carmilla". The process involved close reading of the text and analysing the author's writing style and habits. It also functions as a character analysis, as it is written from the perspective of the protagonist. The project required considerable editing as I worked to make it as true to the original content as possible.
